Luxe

Your Ultimate Terminal-Based YouTube Client, Written in OCaml

About Luxe

Luxe is a powerful and intuitive terminal-based YouTube client developed in OCaml. It provides a seamless way to browse, search, and play YouTube videos directly from your terminal. Designed for efficiency and speed, Luxe is perfect for users who prefer the command line interface.

Features

  • Streamlined Command Line Interface
  • Search and Play YouTube Videos
  • Customizable Themes
  • High Performance and Lightweight
  • Developed in OCaml
